About Us

Welcome to Banco de Melgaço, the only bank where your money is (probably) safe… we think.

Nestled in the heart of Melgaço, a town famous for its exceptional wine, delicious smoked meats, and questionable banking decisions, we take pride in offering financial services as rich as our local flavors—and just as unpredictable!

A Bank Like No Other

Founded by a group of visionaries (a.k.a. people who couldn’t get a job anywhere else), Banco de Melgaço was built on the strong belief that anyone can run a bank, even us! With decades (okay, months) of experience, we’ve mastered the art of almost balancing our accounts while enjoying a fine glass of Alvarinho.

Why Choose Us?

🍷 Wine-Based Rates – Fluctuate with our accountants' drinking habits.

💰 Secure Deposits (Maybe) – Stored safely under the manager’s desk or behind the chorizo.

📉 Random Loans – Flip a coin: heads, you're approved; tails, try again.

📞 Customer Support (Kinda) – Available 10 AM–12 PM, minus lunch breaks.

Our Commitment to You

At Banco de Melgaço, we believe in transparency (especially when it comes to the glass of wine we’re holding). We might not be the most competent bankers, but we sure know how to make finance fun! Whether you’re saving for the future or just trying to understand why your account balance keeps disappearing, we’re here to help—kind of.
